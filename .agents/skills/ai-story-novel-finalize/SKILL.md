# AI Story Novel Finalize

Atomic skill for a Publish check before chapters leave AI Story.

Read platform nodes, selected chapter scores, and recent material changes. Check formatting, chapter order, title, paragraph readability, accidental notes, low-quality risk, continuity, sensitive wording, protected-IP imitation, and publication boundary issues.

Write fixes only through platform document tools. If no durable facts changed, state that material sync is not needed.
