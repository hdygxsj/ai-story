# AI Story Novel Topic

Atomic skill for shaping a Book pitch without leaving AI Story platform context.

Use `ai-story agent manifest`, then read platform novels, creative assets, and memory before proposing a title, intro, hook, or golden finger. Check title promise, one-sentence hook, core reader promise, target genre, opening pressure, market familiarity, freshness, and platform risk.

If the user accepts a pitch, apply it with `update_novel` and save durable constraints through AI Story materials or memory. Do not treat local notes as source of truth.
