# AI Story Novel Worldbuilding

Atomic skill for turning setting into platform material.

Read platform creative assets, map locations, inventory, and timeline. A World rule must state what is possible, what it costs, who benefits, who is harmed, how readers see it in action, and what it cannot solve.

Use `create_world_rule`, `update_creative_asset`, `upsert_map_location`, `upsert_inventory_item`, and timeline tools. Every accepted rule should create pressure, choice, obstacle, or reward in chapters.
