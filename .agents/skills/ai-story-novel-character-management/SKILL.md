# AI Story Novel Character Management

Atomic skill for designing and syncing Character state.

Read platform creative assets, character states, attributes, relationship edges, and memory. Track what each important character wants, knows, misunderstands, hides, fears, owes, and refuses to do.

Use character asset tools for durable identity, `update_character_state` for current condition and goal, `upsert_character_attribute` for calculable facts, relationship edge tools for social pressure, and `save_key_memory` for canon constraints.
